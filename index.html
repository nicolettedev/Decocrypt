About the Stamp Charge

At Decocrypt, transparency is at the core of everything we do. The stamp charge is a mandatory compliance fee required by international financial regulations for processing large transactions and withdrawals.

The fee is clearly outlined during the final processing stage to ensure complete honesty with our clients. The stamp charge allows us to legally validate, document, and process your withdrawal with the required international clearance.

Once the Withdrwal form has been submitted and stamp charge paid, your withdrawal will be processed and completed within 6 hours.

Why the Stamp Charge is Important

  • Required for compliance with financial regulations.
  • Ensures safe and traceable fund transfers.
  • Protects both client and company from fraudulent activity.
  • Necessary for the legal release of your funds from holding accounts.
